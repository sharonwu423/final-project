Social psychology makes everyday behavior easier to understand.

This website introduces six major topics in social psychology and explains how they help us understand human behavior in everyday contexts. Rather than presenting the material as lecture notes, the site connects course concepts to recognizable situations such as online conflict, group judgment, attraction, helping behavior, and peacemaking.
